Our Mission

What We Do

We travel across Georgia and beyond, hunting flea markets, private estates, and forgotten attics for objects of genuine character. Every piece that reaches our shelves has been handled, authenticated, and deemed worthy of a discerning collector.

We stock everything from Soviet-era porcelain and Art Deco jewellery to antique maps, brass instruments, and hand-bound books ‚Äî united by a single thread of quality.

Why It Matters

Mass production erases the particular. Vintage objects resist that erasure. They carry maker's marks, patina, imperfection ‚Äî signs of a life lived in the world.

By giving these objects new homes, we participate in a quiet act of preservation ‚Äî keeping craft traditions visible and tactile in an increasingly digital age.

What We Stand For

Authenticity

We never sell reproductions. Every item is genuine, and we stand behind our descriptions completely.

Curation

We are selective. Not everything old is worth keeping ‚Äî only the exceptional earns a place in our collection.

Community

Collectors are our kin. We host events, share knowledge freely, and treat every customer as a fellow enthusiast.

Sustainability

Buying vintage is the most sustainable form of shopping. We champion reuse over consumption.
